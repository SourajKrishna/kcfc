About KCFC

Kalpetta City FC is a football club founded by Anwas Rasheed in the year 2020. The club is based in Kalpetta, a vibrant town located in the scenic Wayanad district of Kerala, India. Anwas Rasheed's vision and passion for football led to the establishment of this club, with the aim of promoting the sport and nurturing young talent in the region. Since its inception, Kalpetta City FC has emerged as a prominent football club in the local football scene. The club strives to provide a platform for aspiring footballers to showcase their skills, develop their talent, and compete at various levels. Through dedicated training programs and professional coaching staff, Kalpetta City FC focuses on the holistic development of its players, both on and off the field. The club has actively participated in regional tournaments and leagues, consistently achieving commendable results. Kalpetta City FC's style of play emphasizes teamwork, discipline, and a commitment to excellence. The players are instilled with a strong work ethic and a passion for the beautiful game, which is reflected in their performances on the field. Kalpetta City FC aims to create a strong footballing culture in the region by fostering a supportive community and engaging with local fans. The club organizes grassroots initiatives, community outreach programs, and youth development programs to encourage participation and nurture talent from a young age. As the founder, Anwas Rasheed continues to lead and guide the club with his unwavering dedication and love for the sport. His vision is to establish Kalpetta City FC as a renowned football club not just within the region but also at the national level. Under his leadership, the club strives to achieve new heights and create a lasting legacy in the world of football. Kalpetta City FC is more than just a football club; it is a symbol of passion, unity, and the unyielding spirit of its players, staff, and fans. With every match, the club aims to inspire and bring joy to its supporters while making a significant contribution to the growth and development of football in the Kalpetta region and beyond.
